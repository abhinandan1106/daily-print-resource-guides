Weekly Planner and Daily To-Do List Guide

A weekly planner printable gives you the whole week at a glance, while a daily to-do list gives one day the extra detail it needs. Used together, they create a simple paper planning system for school, work, errands, appointments, and home routines.

The Daily Print Project offers a free Printable Weekly Planner and Printable Daily To-Do List for focused task planning.

Simple planning workflow

  1. Print the weekly planner at the start of the week.
  2. Add appointments, deadlines, meal notes, and recurring routines.
  3. Choose the busiest days and print a daily to-do list for each.
  4. Move unfinished tasks forward instead of rewriting the whole week.
  5. Review the week before planning the next one.
