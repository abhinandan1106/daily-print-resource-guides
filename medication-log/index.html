Printable Medication Log Guide

A printable medication log helps organize medicine names, doses, times, refill notes, and questions for appointments. It is useful for daily routines, caregiver handoffs, family binders, and keeping a clearer record when instructions change.

The Daily Print Project offers a free Printable Medication Log for tracking routine medicine details in a simple table.

A medication tracker is an organization tool, not medical advice. Always follow instructions from a doctor, pharmacist, or care team.

Simple setup workflow

  1. Print a fresh medication log.
  2. Write each medicine name clearly with dose and time.
  3. Add refill dates, pharmacy notes, and questions to ask at appointments.
  4. Update the page when a medicine or dose changes.
  5. Keep the completed page in a private but easy-to-find place.
