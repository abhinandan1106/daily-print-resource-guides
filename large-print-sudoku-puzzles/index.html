Large Print Sudoku Puzzles Printable Guide

Large print sudoku puzzles are useful when standard puzzle grids feel too small for comfortable solving. A larger grid gives more room for notes, clearer numbers, and an easier reading experience at a desk, kitchen table, classroom, or senior activity session.

The Daily Print Project has a dedicated large print sudoku puzzles printable page, plus a main printable sudoku puzzle hub with easy, medium, hard, and large-print options.

When large print sudoku helps

  • Daily puzzle practice for solvers who prefer bigger numbers.
  • Senior center activity pages and family puzzle tables.
  • Classroom logic worksheets that need a clean grid.
  • Printable sudoku pages where notes and pencil marks need more space.
  • Low-distraction puzzle sheets for relaxing offline screen breaks.
