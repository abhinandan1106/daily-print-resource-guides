Printable Emergency Contact List Guide

A printable emergency contact list gives a household one clear place for important phone numbers. It is helpful for families, babysitters, caregivers, older adults, renters, and anyone who wants emergency details available without searching through a phone.

The Daily Print Project offers a free Printable Emergency Contact List that can be posted near a phone, placed on the refrigerator, or saved in a family binder.

What to include

  • Immediate family and trusted nearby contacts.
  • Doctors, pharmacy, dentist, and insurance contacts.
  • School, daycare, babysitter, caregiver, and pet care numbers.
  • Utility, landlord, property manager, or building contacts.
  • Short household notes that someone may need quickly.
