Sudoku Difficulty Printable Guide

Printable sudoku puzzles are easier to enjoy when the difficulty level matches the solver. Easy sudoku is best for quick practice, medium sudoku adds more logic, hard sudoku gives experienced solvers a stronger challenge, and large print sudoku improves readability.

The Daily Print Project offers Daily Printable Sudoku Puzzles plus focused pages for Easy Printable Sudoku, Medium Printable Sudoku, and Hard Printable Sudoku.

Choose a sudoku page

  • Easy sudoku: beginner-friendly and faster to finish.
  • Medium sudoku: balanced challenge for regular practice.
  • Hard sudoku: fewer starting numbers and stronger logic work.
  • Large print sudoku: bigger numbers and a more readable printed grid.
