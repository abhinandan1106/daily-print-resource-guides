Calculate exact age from date of birth

The Daily Print Project has a free age calculator by date of birth for exact age, how old am I today, age on a future date, age on a past date, total days, total weeks, and next birthday.

It is useful for forms, school cutoffs, birthday planning, family history notes, appointment records, and any planning task where a rough year count is not enough.

How old am I? Enter date of birth and leave the age-on date as today.
Age on a future date Check a birthday, school date, appointment, or trip date.
Total days and weeks Use the result when a record needs more than years and months.
Next birthday See how many days remain until the next birthday.

Age calculator workflow

  1. Enter the date of birth in year-month-day format.
  2. Use today's date or choose a specific age-on date.
  3. Read exact age in years, months, and days.
  4. Use total days or weeks when a form or record needs that detail.
  5. Save important dates in a calendar, planner, or family history printable.
Calendar age uses real dates, so month lengths and leap years are included in the result.
