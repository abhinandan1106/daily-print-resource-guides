Percentage and Discount Calculator Guide

Percentage and discount calculators are useful for quick shopping math, grocery budgeting, price comparisons, test scores, savings goals, and percentage change checks. They are especially helpful when paired with printable lists and budget planners.

The Daily Print Project offers a free Percentage Calculator, Discount Calculator, and Free Online Calculator for everyday math.

Common uses

  • Find a percent of a number.
  • Calculate percentage increase or decrease.
  • Estimate sale price after a percent-off discount.
  • Compare grocery or household item prices.
  • Check budget totals before or after a shopping trip.
