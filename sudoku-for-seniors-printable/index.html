Why large print sudoku works well for seniors

The Daily Print Project has free large print sudoku puzzles for paper solving. The format is useful for senior centers, home activity folders, caregiver visits, quiet afternoons, and printed puzzle binders.

Readable grids make the puzzle easier to scan, and answer pages make it simple to check progress without needing an app or login.

Readable grids Larger numbers and roomier layouts help make the page easier to use.
Paper solving Print once, solve with pencil, and keep puzzle pages in a binder.
Activity folders Useful for senior centers, family visits, waiting rooms, and quiet time.
Answer support Include solutions so the puzzle can be checked without frustration.

Simple senior puzzle routine

  1. Print one or two large print sudoku pages at a comfortable difficulty.
  2. Use pencil so numbers can be changed while solving.
  3. Start with easy sudoku for warm-up and move to medium puzzles later.
  4. Keep the answer page nearby or in a separate folder.
  5. Add crosswords, word search, or number search for variety.
For activity programs, print several copies and keep answer sheets separate until the end of the session.
